## ABOUT:

Software engineer and founder building **AI-integrated business management systems** for small and medium enterprises in Nigeria.

I run **[Unearth Tech Ltd](https://unearth.ng)**, where I design and deploy web applications and business management systems for clients across hospitality, retail, food service, and related industries.

My current focus is integrating large language models into production business systems — building AI layers that handle operational analysis, automated insight generation, predictive analytics, intelligent reporting, conversational data access, and decision support. The goal is to turn the data businesses already collect into actual decisions, without requiring technical expertise from the user.

## CURRENT FOCUS:

- 🔭 Scaling AI-enhanced business platforms for Nigerian SMEs
- 🎯 Research interests: predictive analytics, intelligent automation, and applied AI for business decision-making
- 🌱 Currently building: production AI integrations using Claude API
