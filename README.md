# Hi, I'm Precstone 👋

I'm a software engineer and founder building **AI-integrated business management systems** for small and medium enterprises in Nigeria.

## What I do

For the past 7 years I've been designing, building, and deploying web applications and business management systems for SMEs across hospitality, retail, food service, and related industries. I founded **Unearth Tech Ltd** in 2019 during my first year of university, and formally incorporated the company with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria in 2024.

My current work focuses on embedding **conversational AI** (Claude API) and automated analytics directly into production business systems — replacing static dashboards with natural-language interfaces that let non-technical owners query their operations and receive insights as conversations rather than tables.
